Population Infographic

Gathering information

I went on the .Gov website and gather the most relevant and up to date and information I could. The latest I could get was 2015 (that still had a large amount of information I could use and make graphically).

Research

I have done infographics in the past so I already had research on them but I decided to refresh my memory before starting my design.

Drawing

Drawing I had an idea in my head of how I wanted my infographic to look so I made a quick wireframe sketch to plan it out and make sure it flowed.

Designing

I made all the elements in one document and then put everything together in another, this allowed me to had more room to work with and to have a less cramped workspace overall.
