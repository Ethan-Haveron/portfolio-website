Bauhaus Poster

Coming up with an idea

I knew everyone was going to do a similar design in their poster but I didn't want my poster to be a carbon copy of the rest of the class so I took a chance and did some research on the furniture such as desks and 1 seated chairs.

Drawing

After seeing some chairs and desks online I decided to draw one out and create a mockup poster with the desk as the main element which would be placed in the middle..

Designing

I made the desk in Illustrator and went through some fonts that match the theme of the poster.
