Meat In A Bap

The Brief

The Previous Meat in a bap website was horrible, it didn’t work on chrome properly, the images were all broken and the layout wasn’t the best either. I took it upon myself to do a complete remake of the website and attempt to make it modern and certainly more user friendly.

Research and wireframe

I always do my research before I start designing anything, it gets me thinking about innovative ideas and how to merge my existing ideas with things I’ve seen online.

I finally came up with an idea that I was happy with, taking my influence from different websites into account I started making the wireframes. Doing this kept me on task and gave me something to stick by when I began coding.

Coding and adding content

The coding part is the best part, something about typing a few lines of code at a time and seeing the result and how the page has changed it fantastic!

Writing the content wasn’t too difficult as more of the copy was on the website. I just had to…spice it up a little and make it more appealing to viewers.

The finished site

I’m super happy with how it turned out! It looks far better from their old site that just wouldn’t work at all. I have learned other skills since then so I would obviously go back and change various parts to make it more appealing to customers and viewers of the site.
