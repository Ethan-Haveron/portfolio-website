Iphone wallpaper

The Brief

I was getting sick of seeing the same old wallpapers on phones. I did find ones that I liked but they were just too crowded and it felt like the screen with the apps on it would look cluttered.

Thus, I sent out to make a minimal wallpaper that would be displayed in the centre of the screen.

Drawing

I have a graphic tablet for my sketches when I donâ€™t have my sketch book handy. I started off by looking at single element wallpapers to help give me ideas on what I could create.

I decided to create and draw an ice lolly using simple shapes such as an oval and rectangle, once I came up with the general size and shape I wanted I brought it over to Illustrator.

Designing

I had my drawing overlaid on top so making the lolly in Illustrator was simple. Once the basic shape was made I wanted to add something more so I put some shading on it so it made the lolly look to have a drip on it.

Finished product

A super straightforward design that sits in the centre of the screen.
