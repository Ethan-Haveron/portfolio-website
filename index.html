Hello!
I'm Ethan

Can you guess my name yet? I am currently a student at Belfast School of Art studying interaction design.
I am an illustrator enthusiast and an ever-improving code dabbler that enjoys bringing ideas to life.

When I am not in University or at home working, I have a group of friends that likes to go on ‘adventures’in wooded areas or places that we know we would get lost in…it’s no fun knowing where you are going.

My plan for next year? I hope to have organised a placement with a design company and do some fantastic work with great people, while learning new skills on the way. You never know where it could take you.

I am a frequent user of Adobe suite and have used and learned the vast number of programmes they have available.

Illustration

I commonly use adobe Illustrator within my day to day actives, both university work and client work.

Web

I have created multiple websites in the past from my teaching of HTML, CSS and JS, thus allowing me to freely create websites how I want them to look and not feel limited within design. Everything is heading to the web and it is an important skill to have under my wing.

Print layout and design

I have done print work such as flyers and leaflets for clients in the past with positive response and feedback from viewers. I enjoy getting away from web design and putting my mind to print, something about being able to physically see the finished piece of work is so satisfying.

Branding

You see a lot of small business out there trying to brand themselves without really knowing what they are looking for. I have the skills to create and improve existing brands to make them be what they were fist imagined as.
